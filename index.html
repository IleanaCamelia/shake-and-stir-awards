Color Trilogy

Roses Street

50 ml gin, 20 ml rose syrup, 20 ml fresh lemon juice, 2 dashes of rose water.

Velvet Sunset Cocktail

40 ml dark rum , 30 ml mango juice, 20 ml strawberry purée, 10 ml maple syrup.

The Shaker’s Spirit

Amethyst Dream

Stirred Elegance

Crimson Reverie

Citrus Symphony

Shake & Stir Awards 2025

The Shake & Stir Awards is a celebration of the finest in mixology, creativity, and craftsmanship. This prestigious event brings together talented bartenders and cocktail enthusiasts from around the world for a thrilling competition that blends innovative techniques with timeless classics.

The Shake & Stir Awards will span over 10 exciting days in June, where competitors will have the chance to showcase their creativity and mixology skills. The event will take place at XHAMAN, Palma de Mallorca, with competitors presenting their signature cocktails after dinner each evening. The jury will then evaluate and give their verdict on each performance.

Daily Schedule

Time Event
7:00 PM Competition begins - Competitors present their signature cocktails
9:00 PM Dinner break
10:00 PM Judging begins - Jury evaluates competitors' cocktails
11:30 PM Final verdicts announced
12:00 AM Event ends for the day

Details for each competition day and category will be updated soon.

Join us for 10 exciting days in June 2025 at XHAMAN, Palma de Mallorca, by the sea. It's a unique opportunity to explore the most inventive cocktails, experience unforgettable presentations, and learn from the best in the industry.
